About Us

The Houston Police Department in TLRP mirrors the real HPD's commitment to community safety, but through immersive roleplay scenarios. From high-speed pursuits to community outreach, every shift counts. Led by Josh Sanders as the Chief of Police, HPD is focused and determined on being a great department and delivering great RP.
